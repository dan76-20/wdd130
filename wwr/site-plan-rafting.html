Overview

Purpose

Our purpose increase bookings for rafting excursions on the rivers. We can do this by showing the types of experiences they may have while on a rafting excursion. We can also increase bookings by showing how much more active a participant they may be during a rafting trip unlike in other activities such as fishing. We can drive revenue and bookings by offering group rates and differing lengths of trips.

Audience

Our customers are people who want to explore the outdoors but may not have the equipment or know how to do it on their own. Many of our customers will be in their early to mid 30's who have established a family and a career. They will be people who don't have as much time as they would like to spend outdoors with their family as they would like and would like to get away from all distractions to spend quality time with each other.They are people who may need a guide to help them navigate the river and to make sure that they will have everything that they need in order to make the trip a success. Our guides will have expert knowledge in the areas of the river being traveled and will be able to assure all members of the group that they are safe at all times. Our target audience are those who want to experience river rafting with their friends and families while leaving the hard work to those who know the area. Many of our target audience will access our site through a desktop PC at work while they dream of getting away from work and all of lifes distractions.
