Welcome Gamer

Welcome Gamers! Did you know that over a quarter of all video game players are over the age of 45? Amazing isn’t it? To many people think of games as being for children, well those people are wrong. Gaming knows no age. A game doesn’t care how old you are. A game doesn’t care how much time you have to play. Games are there for anyone that wants to pick up a controller or put their hands on a keyboard and mouse. Games are many things. They are thought provoking, head scratching puzzles, a means of telling a wonderful story, or portals to another world! Imagine yourself as a Barbarian warrior fighting your way through hoards of enemies to slay that dragon. Or maybe you see yourself as a detective trying to solve a crime by examining the many clues that you find surrounding yourself. With video games, there is no limit other than your imagination. Don’t let your fears of technology stand in your way, come and join us. Find that new world and make new friends. So many worlds need you. Hero, adventure awaits.
