Join the Club

If you would like to come in and check the Golden Games Center out and meet some of the employees and other members, just provide us with your name, phone number and a time you would like to come in to visit the center and we would be happy to show you around. You will be able to see the gaming centers, the wonderful cafeteria, and the library full of comfortable seats and plenty of books to read. Those ready to sign up we do ask a $10 per month donation in order to help keep the Center up and running and in good condition. Most of our expenses come in the form of donations from multiple technology and software companies, Microsoft, Sony, EA, and Blizzard, just to name a few. We understand that for some the $10 is not possible as they are on a very tight budget, and that is why it is not a fee. If it is not doable, we understand and you are more than welcome to join us anyhow.
