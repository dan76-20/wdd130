Golden Gamers Site Plan

Daniel Marks

WDD 130-08

Overview

Purpose

I am an avid video gamer and the son of aging parents. People that grew up playing video games are now starting to reach their senior years and retirement has left many without the daily mental stimulation and socialization that every person needs. Many studies have shown the benefits of Seniors playing video games yet many do not have the money to spend on them or the knowledge of how to get started. It seems like a Senior center outfitted with gaming equipment would solve these issues.

Audience

Our audience is the Senior community and their families. We want to show them that games are not just for their grandchildren but are for them as well. Overcoming a lack of convidence and breaking down barriers is one of our major goals in this website. We want all seniors to understand there is stigma other than that in their own mind that will prevent them from playing and enjoying themselves. Once we overcome the stigma and lack of confidence we can show them how much fun it can be in order to get together with other like minded seniors and game together. A small monthly membership fee would be used to keep technology up to date and in functional state so that they don't even have to worry about the upfront costs and setup that would be required to have the same technology in their own home. Our staff and volunteers would always be on hand in order to help them with any questions or assistance they need.
